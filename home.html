Ben Daws' Homepage

I am a 14 year old developer who mostly works on C and C++ projects. I also created linuxfornoobs.org.

My Internet History

I've had a prescence on the Internet since 2015, when I made a Scratch account. I learned Lua in 2018 before moving to Python, C++, C, A little bit of Rust, and I have since then been scripting using Bash, C, and C++.

My earliest project was a small LFS system that I have since lost to time. It was not a very good LFS build, but at least it was one.

I currently maintain and update linuxfornoobs.org. It's designed to make a Linux installation process easy and simple without ads or random information.

Why is my website in Plain Text?

This website was built about 8 years ago (2016) when I was new to HTML5 in general. While it was not hosted on bendaws.net, it was still a beginner website. Plain text also renders much better across browsers compared to difficult CSS patterns. You can even check out my CSS file to see how basic it is compared to larger, more complete homepages.
